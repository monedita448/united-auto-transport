Why Choose Open Trailer Car Shipping?

Open auto transport is the industry standard for shipping everyday vehicles — and for good reason. When you ask about open car shipping cost, you're tapping into the largest, most active carrier network in the country. Multi-car trailers travel every major interstate corridor daily, which means our open car carrier services can typically get your vehicle picked up faster, and at a lower price, than any enclosed alternative.


For drivers comparing affordable auto transport companies, open transport delivers the best balance of price and reliability. Because open carriers haul six to ten vehicles per trip, the cost of the run is spread across every customer on board — savings that get passed directly to you. Whether you need state to state car shipping open carrier service for a short regional move, or cross country auto transport open trailer service coast to coast, wide carrier availability means shorter wait times and more flexible pickup windows.


A quick call gets you a real, current-market price built on live shipping-lane data — not guesswork, and no bait-and-switch pricing once a driver is en route to pick up your vehicle.


Open transport is the right fit for daily drivers, SUVs, trucks, and most standard vehicles making a residential move, a dealership transfer, or a seasonal relocation. Your vehicle rides secured on a steel-frame trailer alongside other vehicles, exposed to weather and road debris — a tradeoff most owners are glad to make in exchange for meaningfully lower shipping costs and faster carrier assignment.
