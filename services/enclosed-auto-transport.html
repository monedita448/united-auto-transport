Maximum Protection with Enclosed Vehicle Transport

Built for Vehicles That Can't Be Replaced

When you're shipping a vehicle that can't be replaced, standard transport isn't good enough. Enclosed auto transport is engineered explicitly for collectors, luxury car owners, and vintage vehicle enthusiasts who need more than a lower price — they need certainty.


Searching for enclosed car shipping near me usually means you already know what's at stake: paint, provenance, and value that a chipped windshield or a hailstorm can undo in seconds. Our luxury car transport services fully enclose your vehicle in a hard-sided or soft-sided trailer, sealing it off from road debris, weather, and prying eyes for the entire trip.


As an exotic car shipping company, we assign specialized equipment based on what your vehicle actually needs. Low-clearance sports cars ride safely thanks to hydraulic lift-gates that eliminate the risk of scraping a splitter or front lip on a standard ramp. Soft-tie strapping systems secure the vehicle without the wheel-well stress that chain tie-downs put on a factory suspension.


We know pricing is a real consideration — an enclosed vehicle transport cost typically runs 40-60% higher than open transport, reflecting the dedicated equipment, lower vehicle count per trailer, and elevated insurance coverage. That premium buys white glove auto transport: single-car or low-capacity carrier assignments, structural insurance limits well above the industry floor, and drivers who specialize in exactly this kind of cargo.


Whether you're moving a numbers-matching classic or a modern exotic, classic car transport enclosed trailer service is built around one priority: your vehicle arrives in the exact condition it left.
