Information We Collect

When you call, request a callback, or submit our quote form, we collect what you provide: name, phone number, and — if you use the full quote calculator — route, vehicle details, and preferred shipping date. If you arrive from an ad or search result, we also record the source (via UTM parameters) so we know which channel to credit.

How We Use It

Your information is used to connect you with a licensed carrier and to have a specialist call you back, generally within minutes during business hours. Call recordings and tracking numbers may be used for quality and attribution purposes through our call-tracking provider.

Analytics & Tracking

We use analytics and advertising tools (including Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta, and call-tracking software) to understand site usage and measure ad performance. These tools may set cookies or use dynamic phone numbers in your browser.

Sharing

We share quote details with the FMCSA-licensed carrier assigned to your shipment. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information by calling (855) 555-0199.
