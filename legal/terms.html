1. Broker, Not Carrier

United Auto Transport ("Company") operates as a licensed property broker arranging vehicle transportation through third-party, FMCSA-licensed motor carriers. The Company does not itself transport vehicles.

2. Quotes & Pricing

Prices discussed by phone or generated through our quote calculator are estimates based on current market data for the specified route, vehicle, and timing. Final pricing is confirmed in writing before a carrier is dispatched.

3. Carrier Assignment

The Company vets carriers for active DOT/MC authority and current cargo insurance before assignment. Carrier performance and timing can vary due to factors outside the Company's control, including weather, traffic, and mechanical delays.

4. Customer Responsibilities

Customers agree to provide accurate vehicle and contact information, ensure reasonable access for pickup and delivery, and complete the Bill of Lading inspection at both ends of transit.
