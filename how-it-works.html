How It Works

Your Seamless 3-Step Car Shipping Journey

Shipping your vehicle doesn't have to be stressful. We've streamlined the entire auto transport process into three simple, transparent steps. From sourcing a highly rated carrier to final delivery at your door, we handle all the logistics so you can focus on your move.

1

Request Your Quote & Plan Your Route

Getting started takes less than two minutes.

Call now or leave your number for a callback — or use our simple, transparent quote calculator if you'd rather enter details yourself: origin and destination, your vehicle's year, make, and model, its running condition, and your preferred shipping date.


Our system analyzes live market data across nationwide shipping lanes to provide you with a realistic, highly competitive price. No hidden fees, no bait-and-switch pricing — just an honest estimate tailored to your specific vehicle and schedule.

  • Choose your transport type: Open for standard, cost-effective shipping, or Enclosed for white-glove protection for luxury, exotic, or classic cars.
  • Flexible scheduling: Tell us when your vehicle will be ready, and we'll begin mapping out the optimal dispatch window immediately.
2

Custom Carrier Vetting & Route Assignment

We lock in the industry's top 5% of licensed carriers.

Once you approve your quote, our experienced dispatch team goes to work as your dedicated logistical advocate. We don't just post your car to a random board and walk away; we actively source, negotiate, and secure space with premium carriers traveling your exact route.


Every single carrier we partner with undergoes a rigorous, manual screening process before they are allowed to touch your keys.

  • Strict compliance verification: we directly verify active DOT and MC authority via the FMCSA database.
  • Comprehensive insurance auditing: a current certificate of insurance is required directly from the carrier's underwriter.
  • Historical performance checks: safety ratings, promptness records, and past customer reviews, reviewed before assignment.

Once a carrier is assigned, we send you a complete dispatch confirmation containing your driver's name, phone number, and a precise pickup window.

3

Secure Pickup, Transit Tracking & Door-to-Door Delivery

Continuous updates until your keys are back in your hand.

On pickup day, your assigned driver will meet you as close to your residential front door as safely and legally possible. Together, you'll perform a structural inspection of the vehicle, documenting its condition on the Bill of Lading before it is carefully loaded.


During transit, your vehicle is protected by the carrier's cargo insurance policy and backed by our dedicated support team.

  • Real-time progress: direct communication lines to your driver plus tracking updates through our logistics desk.
  • Safe delivery and final inspection: a walk-around at drop-off confirms the vehicle arrived in the exact same condition it left.
  • Easy sign-off: sign the digital Bill of Lading, settle any remaining balance with the driver, and take back your keys.

Why Choose a Trusted Broker?

The auto transport market is highly fragmented, with thousands of independent truck drivers operating across the country. As your broker, we act as your logistical shield — vetting the drivers, verifying insurance, handling the paperwork, and leveraging our industry relationships to ensure your car gets top-tier treatment from start to finish.
